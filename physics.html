2. Kinetic Particle Model of Matter

The Kinetic Particle Model explains that all matter is made up of particles in constant motion. The speed and behavior of these particles vary between solids, liquids, and gases.

Key Points:

  • Particles are always moving
  • Higher temperature means faster particle movement
  • Spaces between particles increase from solids to liquids to gases
