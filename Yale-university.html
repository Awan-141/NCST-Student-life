Nasser Center Hosts Yale University Delegation to Discuss AI Research

Published on: November 27, 2024 | By: NCST Staff

The Nasser Center for Research and Development in Artificial Intelligence, part of the Nasser Scientific and Technical Center, welcomed a delegation of researchers and students from the Jackson Institute for Global Affairs at Yale University during their visit to Bahrain.

Key Highlights:

  • Showcased Bahrain's pioneering efforts in AI applications
  • Discussed recent breakthroughs in generative AI, computer vision, and language models
  • Emphasized the importance of international scientific collaboration

During the tour, the team was briefed on Bahrain's pioneering efforts in AI applications, including the creation of AI solutions for different public and private sector organizations, and keeping up with the most recent breakthroughs in the area, such as generative AI, computer vision, and massive language models to create smart solutions in a variety of industries.

The two sides also highlighted the necessity of scientific collaboration and the sharing of skills in this sector, as well as information transfer between worldwide universities and research institutes, in order to boost innovation and develop AI solutions to serve and improve diverse industries.

This visit underlines Bahrain's regional leadership in AI research and development, as well as the interest of worldwide academic institutions in learning more about the Bahraini experience. The center's goal is to enhance scientific communication and position the Kingdom as a hub for cultural and economic interaction, therefore contributing to the growth of the innovation and creative ecosystem on both a local and worldwide scale.
